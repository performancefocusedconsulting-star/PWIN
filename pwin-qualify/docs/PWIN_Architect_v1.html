How to Use PWIN Architect
A capture-phase coaching tool for pursuit teams. Read this before your first session.
The right mindset
PWIN Architect is a coaching tool, not a reporting tool. Its purpose is to surface the gaps in your pursuit position so you can close them — not to produce a number that satisfies a governance gate. Score yourself honestly. A team that scores 35% and knows exactly why is in a far stronger position than a team that claims 70% on thin evidence.

The AI Assurance Review is a challenge mechanism, not a validation stamp. Complete your scoring independently first. Only then use the AI to pressure-test your evidence. If you adjust scores before the AI review to improve the result, you are coaching the tool — not yourself.
Step-by-step workflow
1
Score independently
Go to the Assessment tab. For each of the 24 questions, select your honest position — Strongly Agree, Agree, Disagree, or Strongly Disagree. Leave blank only if the question genuinely cannot be assessed yet. Blank counts as zero and will pull your PWIN score down, which is intentional.
2
Record your evidence
For each scored question, enter your supporting evidence in the text box below the dropdown. Be specific: name the individuals involved, state their role, reference documents, and describe commitments made. Vague evidence produces a weak AI review. The AI cannot challenge what it cannot read.
3
Review the PWIN Dashboard
Go to the PWIN Dashboard tab to see your overall score, pursuit phase, radar profile, and category breakdown. This gives the team a shared picture of where the pursuit is strong and where attention is needed before the AI review session.
4
Run the AI Assurance Review
Go to the AI Assurance Review tab. Select each question from the left panel and click Run AI Assurance Review. The AI will assess whether your evidence justifies your score, flag inflation, and provide challenge questions. Once you have reviewed key questions, generate a Full Assurance Report for a gate-level synthesis.

Tips for effective use
◎
Use it regularly
PWIN Architect is most valuable as a recurring coaching session — ideally monthly during capture. Track how your PWIN score moves over time as the pursuit develops.
⚠
Blank = honest, not lazy
If you genuinely have not yet built a position on a question, leave it blank. Do not select Disagree to avoid a zero — that obscures the real gap. Zeros are flags for action.
◈
Run the AI review after scoring
Always complete and commit your scores before visiting the AI Assurance Review tab. The review is a challenge to your independent assessment, not a guide to what score to choose.
✓
Evidence is everything
The AI review quality scales directly with the quality of your evidence. Single sentences get weak challenges. Specific named evidence — who said what, when, in what context — produces actionable coaching output.
