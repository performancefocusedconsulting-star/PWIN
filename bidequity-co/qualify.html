Qualification criteria

What the tool assesses.

01

Incumbent Position

Are you the incumbent, a known challenger, or entering cold? This is the single strongest predictor of outcome.

02

Relationship Depth

The quality and seniority of your existing relationships with the buying organisation and evaluation panel.

03

Solution Readiness

How mature is your technical solution or delivery model for this specific requirement?

04

Competitive Landscape

How many credible competitors, and what is your assessment of their positioning and intent?

05

Pricing Confidence

Do you have a credible view of the target price point and confidence in your ability to be competitive?

06

Strategic Alignment

Does this contract align with your strategic direction, or is it opportunistic pursuit with limited long-term value?

07

Bid Capacity

Do you have the resource capacity and senior commitment to prosecute this pursuit to the standard required?

08

Risk Profile

Contract terms, delivery risk, political sensitivity, and reputational exposure of the opportunity.
