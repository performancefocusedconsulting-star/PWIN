The pursuit lifecycle

Four capability areas. One integrated methodology.

Our methodology spans the full pursuit lifecycle — from the decision to compete through to post-outcome learning. Each capability area is structured, repeatable, and governed by data.

Qualification and Intelligence

The pursuit decision is the highest-leverage moment in the bid lifecycle. We apply forensic scoring to every opportunity — evaluating positioning, competitive landscape, and commercial fit — so you compete where conviction is highest and walk away from the rest.

Pursuit qualification · Opportunity scoring · Competitive intelligence · Market positioning analysis

Strategy and Positioning

Win strategy that starts before the tender lands. We build the intelligence architecture that positions your bid — shaping the narrative, identifying discriminators, and structuring the commercial model to score against evaluation criteria, not just comply with them.

Win strategy architecture · Pre-tender shaping · Pricing strategy · Evaluation alignment

Governance and Delivery

Review gate governance · Compliance assurance · Submission readiness · Decision cadence

Learning and Improvement

Every pursuit generates data. Most organisations discard it. We capture, structure, and compound pursuit performance data — effort versus estimate, PWIN trajectory, and workstream effectiveness — so each engagement makes the next one sharper.

Post-outcome analysis · Performance benchmarking · Capability development · Data compounding

The cost advantage

A fundamental reduction in bid cost.

The combination of structured methodology and platform-enabled capability delivers a step change in what it costs to produce a competitive submission. This is not about writing faster. It is about eliminating the wasted effort, duplicated work, and governance failures that drive up the cost of every bid.

Structured activities compress timelines. Platform automation eliminates manual processes. Forensic qualification stops you spending on pursuits you were never going to win. The cumulative effect is a dramatic reduction in cost of sale — across your entire bid portfolio.

Methodology. Calibrated effort models and dependency maps that eliminate the guesswork, rework, and late-stage chaos that inflates every bid.

Platform. Technology that automates structured activities, surfaces patterns across procurement data, and compresses the manual effort in every workstream.

Qualification. The most expensive bid is the one you should never have started. Forensic scoring eliminates low-probability pursuits before they consume resource.
