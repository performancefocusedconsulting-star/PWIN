About BidEquity

The discipline your bid operation is missing.

A strategic pursuit consultancy that exists to change how organisations compete for major UK public sector contracts.

What BidEquity is

A strategic pursuit consultancy.

BidEquity exists to change how organisations compete for major UK public sector contracts. We bring discipline, intelligence, and pressure-testing to pursuit strategy — replacing the ad-hoc, unmeasured approach that most firms accept as inevitable with a structured methodology that treats every pursuit as a strategic investment.

Our operating model spans the full bid lifecycle — from qualification scoring and win strategy through pricing discipline, compliance assurance, and post-outcome analysis. We bring sector depth in Defence, Justice, Emergency Services, and transformation programmes.

What BidEquity is not

We draw clear lines.

Not SaaS. We are not bid management software. We are practitioners who deploy technology as part of an engagement model.

Not a writing shop. We do not produce proposal text on demand. We build the intelligence and strategy that makes the writing possible.

Not a retainer. Our commercial model is tied to outcomes, not time. We are co-invested in every pursuit we take on.

How we work

The expertise and the system, together.

One without the other is either opinion without structure, or structure without judgement. BidEquity combines deep pursuit expertise with a proprietary methodology platform — consulting-led, platform-enabled.

The methodology is built from two decades of complex programme acquisition. It encodes the specific activities, dependencies, and decision points that determine whether a bid operation produces a winning submission. The platform captures, structures, and compounds this knowledge across every engagement.

Methodology-led

Calibrated effort models, dependency maps, and archetype configurations built from real pursuit experience — not theory.

Platform-enabled

Technology that amplifies the methodology — automating structured activities, surfacing patterns, and compressing the effort required to compete.

Outcome-aligned

Our fees are contingent on contract award. We share the risk and the conviction.

In practice: An Emergency Services programme had been through two failed procurement cycles with different consultancies each time. The third attempt used a single integrated methodology from qualification through submission. The bid team was smaller, the timeline was shorter, and the submission scored highest on every evaluation criterion. Methodology and platform, together.

Commercial model

Paid on Award.

Our fees are contingent on the formal contract award. We absorb the cost of intelligence during the pursuit phase and get paid when you win.

This model exists because it forces the right behaviour. It makes us selective — we will not take on a pursuit we do not believe is winnable. It aligns incentives at the pursuit level, not the timesheet level. And it means every recommendation we make is governed by conviction, not billable hours.

The result is a consultancy that only earns when its judgement proves correct. That is the discipline we bring to your bid operation — and the discipline we apply to our own.

How it works

1. We qualify the opportunity together using our scoring methodology.

2. If we proceed, we deploy our intelligence overlay at a small base cost.

3. The substantive fee is paid only on formal contract award.

4. If you do not win, our investment in your pursuit is absorbed.
