About BidEquity

The discipline your bid operation is missing.

What BidEquity is

A strategic pursuit consultancy.

BidEquity exists to change how organisations compete for major UK public sector contracts. We bring discipline, intelligence, and pressure-testing to pursuit strategy — replacing the ad-hoc, unmeasured approach that most firms accept as inevitable with a forensic methodology that treats every pursuit as a strategic investment.

Our operating model spans the full bid lifecycle — from qualification scoring and win strategy through pricing discipline, compliance assurance, and post-outcome analysis. We bring sector depth in Defence, Justice, Emergency Services, and transformation programmes.

What BidEquity is not

We draw clear lines.

Not SaaS. We are not bid management software. We are practitioners who deploy technology as part of an engagement model.

Not a writing shop. We do not produce proposal text on demand. We build the intelligence and strategy that makes the writing possible.

Not a retainer. Our commercial model is tied to outcomes, not time. We are co-invested in every pursuit we take on.

How we work

The expertise and the system, together.

One without the other is either opinion without structure, or structure without judgement. BidEquity combines deep pursuit expertise with a proprietary methodology platform — consulting-led, platform-enabled.

The methodology is built from two decades of complex programme acquisition. It encodes the specific activities, dependencies, and decision points that determine whether a bid operation produces a winning submission. The platform captures, structures, and compounds this knowledge across every engagement.

The result is a fundamental reduction in what it costs to produce a competitive submission. Not incremental efficiency — a step change in bid economics driven equally by structured methodology and technology-enabled capability.

Methodology-led. A proprietary pursuit methodology that encodes what works — calibrated effort models, dependency maps, and archetype configurations built from real pursuit experience, not theory.

Platform-enabled. Technology that amplifies the methodology — automating structured activities, surfacing patterns across procurement data, and compressing the effort required to produce competitive submissions.

Outcome-aligned. A commercial model where BidEquity invests alongside you. Our fees are contingent on contract award. We share the risk and the conviction.

Commercial model

Paid on Award.

Our fees are contingent on the formal contract award. We absorb the cost of intelligence during the pursuit phase. Our remuneration is mathematically tethered to your success.

This model creates total incentive alignment. We do not earn unless you win. That means we are selective about the pursuits we take on — and ruthlessly honest about the ones you should walk away from.

How it works

1. We qualify the opportunity together using our scoring methodology.

2. If we proceed, we deploy our intelligence overlay at a small base cost.

3. The substantive fee is paid only on formal contract award.

4. If you do not win, our investment in your pursuit is absorbed.

Our approach

Forensic. Disciplined. Outcome-led.

We do not compete on volume or speed of proposal production. We compete on the quality of pursuit decisions and the rigour of win strategy. Every engagement is governed by data, structured by methodology, and measured against outcomes.
