Who we are

The pursuit value chain, redesigned.

Complex pursuit production follows the same pattern across the sector: large teams, competing priorities, governance that cannot withstand pursuit pressure. The operating model has not changed in two decades — even as contract values and competitive intensity have increased.

BidEquity has mapped the end-to-end pursuit value chain — 79 structured activities across 10 workstreams. Every activity has a calibrated effort model, dependency map, and archetype configuration. We have redesigned the activities where effort is absorbed without improving win probability.

The alternatives

Why they do not work.

Consultancies bill time, not outcomes. Their model rewards utilisation. They staff your pursuit, invoice monthly, and move on — regardless of result. No compounding. No shared conviction.

SaaS platforms automate workflow, not judgement. They track compliance and manage documents. They do not tell you whether to pursue, how to win, or where your strategy is vulnerable.

Writing shops produce text, not strategy. They are downstream of the decisions that actually determine score — pricing, positioning, discriminator selection. They have no stake in whether the words they write lead to a win.

BidEquity operates across all three dimensions — practitioner experience, AI-enabled platform, and commercial alignment — and breaks the pattern on each.

Commercial model

Paid on Award.

Our fees are contingent on the formal contract award. We absorb the cost of intelligence during the pursuit phase and get paid when you win.

This model forces the right behaviour. It makes us selective — we will not take on a pursuit we do not believe is winnable. Every recommendation is governed by conviction, not billable hours.

A consultancy that only earns when its judgement proves correct. The rigour we apply to our own operation.

How it works

1. We qualify the opportunity together using our scoring framework.

2. If we proceed, we deploy our intelligence overlay at a small base cost.

3. The substantive fee is paid only on formal contract award.

4. If you do not win, our investment in your pursuit is absorbed.
