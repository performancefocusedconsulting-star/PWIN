---
name: Governance gates and production scope
description: G8 only gates quality/solution responses, not all production; many item types can start day 1 without waiting for solution lock
type: feedback
---

G8 (Storyboard Sign-off / Solution Lock) only gates **quality/solution response questions** — the scored technical answers that depend on the solution design being locked. Even those can stagger across the forecasted completion date, not all waiting for a single moment.

**Items that can start on day 1 without waiting for G8:**
- Legal and company information (finalised in ~1 week)
- Insurance review and certificate compilation
- Security and data protection
- Contract review and markup

**Why:** These items don't depend on solution design. Standard industry practice is to start them immediately and get them finished early, freeing capacity for the scored responses.

**How to apply:** The production scheduler must categorise ResponseItems into gated (waits for G8/solution lock) vs ungated (starts immediately). Ungated items get their backward schedule from submission deadline independently of G8. Gated items have G8 as an additional start constraint.

G2 (Solution & Strategy Review) and G3 (Commercial/Finance Review) are **assurance and guidance sessions**, not sign-off gates. Assets need to be relatively mature but not complete. Purpose is to inform thinking and approach. They should be scheduling constraints (assets should be ready enough to review) but not hard blockers in the same way G8 is for quality responses.

Executive/Board committees (G5/G6/G7) are the actual sign-off gates, and typically happen after a shorter run-through of G2 and G3.
