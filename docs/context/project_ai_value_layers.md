## Four-layer AI value model (2026-04-02)

Real bid cost is ~2,000 person-days for a complex services opportunity, not the 505 methodology baseline. The 4x multiplier exists because bids cost by team reservation (team size x duration x day rates), not by productive task effort.

**Layer 1 — Task Efficiency:** 34.1% effort reduction (172 of 505 days). Computed bottom-up. Weakest layer commercially.

**Layer 2 — Timeline Compression:** AI compresses critical path, releasing reserved team 2-3 weeks earlier. 150-225 person-days of reservation cost eliminated per bid.

**Layer 3 — Team Fungibility:** AI as institutional memory. New team members onboard in days not weeks. Specialists serve multiple concurrent bids. Mid-bid team changes are managed transitions. 30% more concurrent pursuit capacity.

**Layer 4 — Decision Velocity (highest value):** AI brings informed go/no-go from month 3 to week 1. Gate 1 gets full ITT analysis, compliance matrix, contract review, competitive landscape, win strategy draft. Bad pursuits killed early. 400-600 person-days avoided per early kill. £720k-2.4m annual cost avoidance for a firm running 20+ pursuits.

**Why:** Layer 4 is the commercial pitch. "How many pursuits did you kill late last year? What did it cost?" PWIN Architect gives that information in week 1.

**How to apply:** When discussing AI value, always lead with Layer 4 (decision velocity) and Layer 3 (team fungibility). Layer 1 (task efficiency) is supporting evidence, not the headline.
