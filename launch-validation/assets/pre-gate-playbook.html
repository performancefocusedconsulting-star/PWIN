The Pre-Gate Pursuit Playbook

Five stages across the 4–5 month capture window. Four workstreams running in parallel.

  • Initial Buyer Need Hypothesis
  • Initial Fit Assessment
S2

Buyer & Field Shaping

  • Buyer Decision Thesis
  • Stakeholder Map
  • Competitor Field Map
  • Incumbent Defensibility Assessment
S3

Win Architecture Design

  • Path to Win Statement
  • Win Themes
  • Proof Map
  • Solution Spine
S4

Commercial Risk & Suitability Review

  • Commercial Stance
  • Suitability Review
  • Risk Redline Summary

S5

Commitment Readiness

  • Gate Pack
  • Formal Recommendation
  • Full Go / Conditional / Watch / No Bid
→ → → Formal Gate → → → Bid Execution

The four workstreams

Running in parallel across all five stages
  1. Buyer & Opportunity Shaping — building the buyer decision thesis, anticipating the procurement.
  2. Competitive & Incumbent Intelligence — mapping the field, assessing the incumbent, identifying displacement leverage.
  3. Solution & Proof Strategy — designing the solution spine, building the proof map, threading win themes.
  4. Commercial Risk & Suitability — sizing the commercial stance, reviewing risk, closing gap-fix actions.

Plus a fifth workstream — Pursuit Readiness & Governance — running across all four to keep the gate pack on track.
