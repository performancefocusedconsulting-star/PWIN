# Source Hierarchy — Sector Intelligence

## Tier 1 — Official Government & Regulatory (highest confidence)
- GOV.UK policy papers, White Papers, Green Papers
- Legislation.gov.uk (Acts and statutory instruments)
- NAO and PAC reports
- Departmental annual reports and spending reviews
- Regulatory publications (CQC, Ofsted, Ofwat, ICO, etc.)
- Cabinet Office procurement policy notes (PPNs)

## Tier 2 — Procurement & Market Data
- Find a Tender Service (FTS) OCDS award data via pwin-platform MCP
- CCS framework award lists and category strategies
- Contract Finder spend data
- Government spend data (Spotlight on Spend, OSCAR)
- HMT Green Book, spending round documentation

## Tier 3 — Trade Press, Analysis & Commentary
- Sector journals: Civil Service World, PublicTechnology, LocalGov, HSJ, DSEI, Justice Gap
- Think tanks: Reform, IPPR, Institute for Government, Nuffield Trust, Kings Fund
- Parliamentary debates, Hansard, written ministerial statements, PQs
- Industry associations: techUK, CSSA, NHS Confederation, LGA
- Analyst/consultant reports (Kroll, Deloitte, PWC public sector publications)
