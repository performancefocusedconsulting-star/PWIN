| NAO / PAC report | S1, S5 | S4, S6 |

| FTS spend data export | S2 | S3 |
| Think tank report | S3, S5 | S4, S6 |
| Trade press article (policy) | S1, S4 | S6 |
| Trade press article (market trend) | S3, S4 | S6 |
| Parliamentary debate / select committee | S1, S4 | S5 |
