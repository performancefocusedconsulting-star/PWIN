# Source Hierarchy — Supplier Intelligence

## Tier 1 — Primary Official (highest confidence)
- Companies House filings (annual reports, confirmation statements, director changes)
- Stock exchange filings (RNS, investor presentations, interim/full year results)
- Regulatory filings (CMA, FCA, SFO, NHS England, Cabinet Office)
- Government procurement award notices

## Tier 2 — Procurement & Market Data
- Find a Tender Service (FTS) OCDS award data via pwin-platform MCP
- CCS framework award lists and lot holder registers
- Crown Commercial Service supplier information pages
- NHS Supply Chain, G-Cloud, DOS framework registers
- Contract Finder award notices

## Tier 3 — Trade Press, Analysis & Signals
- Civil Service World, PublicTechnology, LocalGov, HSJ, Defence News
- LinkedIn (leadership announcements, company updates, job postings)
- Glassdoor (culture and leadership signals — low-confidence)
- Analyst reports (Kroll, PWC, Deloitte public sector benchmarks)
- Think tank publications (Reform, IPPR, Institute for Government)

## Tier 4 — Internal Intelligence (restricted)
- Internal CRM notes and account team observations
- Post-bid debrief notes and evaluator feedback
- Partner or subcontractor intelligence
- Conference/event intelligence

Tier 4 sources must use `SRC-INT-nnn` IDs and are only added in AMEND mode.
